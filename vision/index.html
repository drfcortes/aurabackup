Vision

The AURA protocol envisions a future where AI-generated content is transparently declared and traceable across the academic, journalistic, and creative domains.

  • Foster trust in AI-augmented knowledge production.
  • Reduce misinformation and unverified claims by tagging AI-generated outputs.
  • Enable reproducible workflows with proper attribution.
