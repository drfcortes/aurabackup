The AURA Initiative

AURA (Attribution & Usage Reporting for AI) is an open protocol designed to ensure transparent and standardized reporting of AI-assisted content generation.

  • Declare AI involvement clearly in scientific and public work.
  • Generate verifiable metadata for each AI-assisted contribution.
  • Enable traceability and reproducibility through public identifiers.
